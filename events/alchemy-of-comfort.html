Alchemy of Comfort

September 30, 2025

Diners move through a progression of comforting dishes made strange: truffled dumplings under hay smoke, bitter-sweet stews, and a dessert of chocolate ashes and milk snow. The evening ends with Elias sharing a question, not a speech: “What is comfort to you?”

Chef Elias Müller
